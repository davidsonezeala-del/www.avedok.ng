Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs, admissions, and scholarships.

Applying for a scholarship is easy! Simply create an account, complete your profile, and navigate to the Scholarships section. You'll find all available scholarships with their eligibility criteria. Click "Apply" on any scholarship that matches your profile, complete the application form, and submit the required documents. Our team reviews applications within 2-4 weeks.

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, you'll need: a high school diploma or equivalent (for undergraduate programs), official transcripts, a statement of purpose, letters of recommendation, and English proficiency scores (for non-native speakers). Some programs may require standardized test scores or portfolio submissions.

Our platform features live virtual classrooms, recorded lectures, interactive assignments, and collaborative projects. Some programs offer optional in-person intensives or networking events.

We offer flexible payment options including: full upfront payment (with a 10% discount), monthly installment plans, deferred payment options, and third-party financing through our partner institutions. We accept major credit cards, bank transfers, PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments.
